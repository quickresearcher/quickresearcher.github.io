What is art gobblers ?

Art Gobblers is a decentralized art factory owned by aliens made by Justin Rolland (co-creator of Adult Swim's Rick and Morty) and Paradigm (A well known crypto asset investment firm.). As artists make cool art, Gobblers gains cultural relevance, making collectors want the art more, incentivizing artists to make cooler art. It's also an on-chain game.

What are Gobblers,Goo and Gobbler’s multiple?

Art Gobblers themselves are fully animated ERC1155 NFTs. In particular, they eat art that artists draw using art gobbler’s draw tool and turn into 1/1 NFTs using in-game resources. All the artworks a Gobbler eats belong to it on-chain and are displayed in its belly gallery forever.Goo is an ERC20 Ethereum token emitted by Art Gobblers NFTs and is needed to make new Blank Pages and its supply is quadratically unlimited .Gobbler’s multiple is base speed of gobbler squirting out Goo. Every Gobbler has its own multiple which will determine the amount of goo it can earn.
